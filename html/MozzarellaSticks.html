Mozzarella Sticks



Mozzarella sticks are a cheesy, refreshing goodness able to make your stomach fill with deliciousness. The crispy, breaded outside pairs with the soft, gooey inner cheese which instantly hits your pallet and a tomato marinara sauce.

Ingredients

  • Panko or breadcrumbs
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Mozzarella
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Marinara Sauce (if desired)

Recipe Instructions

    How to Make

  1. Cut the cheese in smaller sticks

  2. Have your flour, beaten eggs, and panko/breadcrumbs in separate bowls for the breading

  3. Coat the cheese in flour, then dip in egg, then coat it in the panko/breadcrumbs

  4. If youâ€™re indulging in extra crispiness, repeat step 3

  5. Freeze the breadcrumbed cheese for 30-60 minutes. This is the key step! It keeps the cheese from melting too fast

  6. Heat your vegetable oil to 350 degrees F or 175 degrees Celcius

  7. Fry the frozen breadcrumbed cheese for 1-2 minutes until golden brown

  8. Drain the fried cheese in paper towels

  9. Enjoy with a side of marinara sauce!
