Jalapeno Popper Quesadillas



Jalapeno Popper Quesadillas are an apetizer made with flour tortillas filled with a cheesy mixture and jalapeno peppers for a little kick in every bite. It is well seasoned with garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper added to the filling to make it a delicious snack.

Ingredients

  • 2 large flour tortillas
  • 2 jalapeno peppers
  • 4 ounces of cream cheese, room temperature
  • 1/2 cups of mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cups of Montrey Jack cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 teaspoons of garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoons of onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoons of kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoons of pepper

Recipe Instructions

    How to Make

  1. Mix together the cream cheese, shredded cheese, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper in a small bowl. Set aside.

  2. Heat a large skillet over the gas stove. Once it's hot, add chopped jalapenos to the skillet to cook.

  3. When done, remove the skillet from the heat.

  4. Add the jalapenos to the cream cheese mixture.

  5. To the same skillet, place a tortilla over medium heat (do not wipe out the skillet).

  6. Add half on the cream cheese filling (about 1/2 cup) to the tortilla. You may need a spatula to help spread out the mixture.

  7. Fold the tortilla in half anf cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until slightly browned. Repeat with the second tortilla and filling.

  8. Cut and fold immediately. Enjoy!
