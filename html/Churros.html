Churros



Churros are a fried delight in many areas! They are crispy and decadent and can be paired with many sweet sauces like chocolate. It’s a dough treat which is shaped in long strips or curved into teardrops or hearts!

Ingredients

    Churros

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 ½ tablespoons sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour

    • Frying

  • Vegetable oil

    • Coating

  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Recipe Instructions

    How to Make

  1. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine water, sugar, salt, and 2 tablespoons of oil. Bring to a boil, then remove from heat. Stir in the flour until the mixture forms a ball.

  2. Heat oil for frying in a deep skillet or pot to about 375°F (190°C).

  3. Spoon dough into a piping bag fitted with a star tip. Pipe strips of dough into the hot oil, cutting with scissors if needed.

  4. Fry until golden and crispy, about 2–4 minutes per batch. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels.

  5. For the coating of the churro, mix sugar and cinnamon in a shallow dish. Roll the warm churros in the mixture to coat it. Enjoy your delicious cinnamon flavored dessert.
