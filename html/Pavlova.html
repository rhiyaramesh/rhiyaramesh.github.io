Pavlova



Pavlova is an Australian meringue-based dessert with a crisp, crunchy exterior, a soft, marshmellowy center, topped with whipped cream and fresh fruit. This recipe will serve 12 people and is the perfect way to finish a meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of white sugar, divided
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • 3 large white eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of lemon juice
  • 1 1/4 cups of heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2 cup of confectioners' sugar
  • 1 pint of fresh strawberries

Recipe Instructions

    How to Make

  1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees Fahrenheit. Line a rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper; draw a 9-inch circle in the center of the paper.

  2. Whisk 1/4 cups of sugar and cornstarch together in a small bowl.

  3. Whip egg whites in a bowl made of glass, metal, or ceramic, until soft peaks form. Slowly incorporate the remaining 3/4 cups of sugar, and keep beating until the sugar has fully dissolved and stiff peaks emerge. Carefully mix the peaks with the cornstarch mixture and lemon juice.

  4. Apply a 1/4 inch thick layer of meringue onto the prepared baking sheet, ensuring it fills the circle. Use a piping bag or spoon to add the remaining meringue, creating a raised border around the circle to form a shallow bowl, and incorporate decorative swirls if you like.

  5. Place in the preheated oven and bake for 1 hour. After baking, turn off the oven and allow the meringue to remain inside for 30 minutes without opening the door. Once the 30 minutes have passed, take the meringue out of the oven; it should have a firm exterior and be slightly soft on the inside.

  7. Enjoy this scrumptious dessert!
