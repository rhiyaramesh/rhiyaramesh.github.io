Lasagna



A lasagna is a savory dish that consists of flat sheets of pasta put together, filled with cheese and/or spinach. Lasagnas are normalluy baked or cooked in a large pot with multiple layers together. It is usually covered in red or white pasta sauce and can be served with some vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles, uncooked
  • 4 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 1/2 cups of shredded paresan cheese. shredded or divided
  • 3/4 teaspoons of salt, according to cheese

    • Tomato Sauce

  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 36 ounces of pasta sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning

    • Cheese Mixture

  • 2 cups ricotta, or cottage cheese
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 large egg, beaten

Recipe Instructions

    How to Make

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the lasagna noodles and cook until firm. Drain, then rinse the noodles under cold water, and set aside.

  2. Meanwhile, in a large skillet or dutch oven, cook the onion and garlicover medium-high heat until it turns brown.

  3. Stir in the pasta sauce, tomato sauce, seasoning, 1/2 teaspoons of salt, and 1/4 teaspoons of black pepper. Simmer with the pot open over medium heat for 5 minutes or until slightly thickened. Taste and season the sauce to match preferred taste.

  4. In a separate medium bowl, mix 1 1/2 cups of mozzarella cheese, 1/4 cups of parmesan cheese, ricotta cheese, parsley, egg, and 1/4 teasopoons of salt.

  5. Spread 1 cup of the sauce in a 9 x 13 pan or casserole dish. Top it with 3 lasagna noodles. Layer with 1/3 of the cheese mixture and 1 cup of sauce. Repeat this twice more. Finish by placing 3 more noodles topped with the remaining sauce.

  6. Cover the lasagna with foild and bake for 45 minutes.

  7. Remove the foil and sprinkle the top of the lasagna with the remaining cheese. Bake it uncovered for an additional 15 minutes or until brown and bubbly. Broil for 2-3 minutes if desired.

  8. Let the lasagna rest for at least 15 minutes before cutting. Enjoy!
