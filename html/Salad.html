Salad



Salad is a healthy dish which consists of many types of vegetables. It is usually combined with salad dressing and some cheese. There are two types: there is a vegetable salad, and there is a fruit salad, which consists only of fruit, while a vegetable salad can also have some fruit.

Ingredients

  • About 4 ounces lettuce or mixed greens, washed, dried, and torn
  • Finely shredded carrots or thin strips of cucumber (optional)
  • Small handful finely shredded fresh basil, mint or other aromatic herb (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons vinegar or lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon honey or maple syrup
  • Flaky salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Grated Parmesan or Asiago cheese, for garnish (optional)

Recipe Instructions

    How to Make

  1. Make sure your greens are dry. Lay your greens out on a towel to air-dry for a little while.

  2. Tear up your greens. Cut them into smaller pieces if they are big.

  3. Toss it in a mixing bowl.

  4. Add grated vegetables and chopped herbs.

  5. Prepare your salad dressing. Dress the salad with vinegar, some lemon juice, honey and some olive oil. Add sweetnesses if desired.

  6. Drizzle your salad with dressing. Coat the greens very, very lightly.

  7. Sprinkle on salt and pepper. Adjust taste if necessary, then serve them in individual bowls. Enjoy!
