Flan



Flan is a tasty, custard dessert with an extremely rich caramel sauce which is popular all across Latin America. Its delicious layers of sugary goodness originated from Spain!

Ingredients

    Caramel

  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) water

    • Custard

  • 1 can (14 oz / 400g) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can (12 oz / 355ml) evaporated milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Recipe Instructions

    How to Make

    Caramel

  1. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine sugar and water. Cook without stirring until it turns a golden amber color (watch closely!). Immediately pour into the bottom of a flan mold or baking dish, tilting to coat the bottom. Let it cool and harden.

    2. Custard

  2. In a blender or bowl, combine sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk, eggs, and vanilla. Blend or whisk until smooth. Pour the mixture over the set caramel in the mold.

    3. Bake

  3. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Place the flan mold inside a larger baking dish. Fill the outer dish with hot water halfway up the sides (water bath). Bake for 50–60 minutes, or until the center is just set (a knife should come out mostly clean).

    4. Chill

  4. Remove from oven, let it cool, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours (overnight is best).

    5. Serve

  5. Run a knife around the edges, then invert onto a plate to release the flan with the caramel sauce on top. Enjoy!
